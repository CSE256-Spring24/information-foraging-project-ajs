Application & Enrollment Procedure

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

Step 1: Apply

Fill out the appliction form for Admission online: Fill out form; or return the pdf version: Application Form pdf

When applying to the nursery school, there's a $40 application fee. Once received, your child will be placed on the enrollment list. Acceptance notifications are sent out in March before the fall enrollment date.

Orientation Day: The school supports children's transition into the school by giving the child and parent(s) an opportunity to visit the classroom and meet the teachers and classmates. This one-hour visit provides an opportunity for children to engage in free play while parents receive a brief overview of what to expect during the school year. The day following orientation, children attend school independently, either on Wednesday or Thursday (refer to the orientation schedule). Classes are divided into two groups, with each group attending on either Wednesday or Thursday, allowing for small group interactions before the entire class convenes on Friday.
